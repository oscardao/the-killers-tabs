The Killers - "Jenny Was a Friend of Mine" 
(Tabbed by nuke126@gmail.com)
-------------------------------------------------------------------
This is the true way to play the song. I watched many live videos 
and this is exactly what Dave Keuning plays. All the other tabs for 
the song are wrong (not being a jackass, it's just the truth). The 
chords may look odd, but Dave played jazz guitar in high school and 
it influenced his playing.
-------------------------------------------------------------------
